What is the relationship between LEGO IDEAS and the BrickLink Designer Program?
LEGO IDEAS sets are fan ideas that are re-imagined and produced by LEGO designers through the standard retail assortment.
They follow the normal LEGO set lifecycle and can retire like any standard LEGO set. BDP sets, by contrast, are designed by fans
and produced in limited crowdfunded runs — they are not part of the standard retail assortment and do not retire in the same
way. If a set reached 10,000 supporters on LEGO Ideas but was NOT approved by LEGO, the designer may submit it to the
BrickLink Designer Program instead. This means some sets that fans associate with LEGO Ideas may actually have ended up as
BDP sets rather than standard IDEAS sets.
